The Game

The aim of this service is to provide users with a way to track thier transportation carbon emmesions while also sharing them with others.. Your entered miles per gallon of your vehicle is then translated into an estimated pounds of co2 emitted per mile driven. When you enter a recent trip pf your your earn postive points for bike travel and negative point for car travel. The point value is approximatly the pounds of co2 you saved from entering the atmosphere. For example if you earn 2 points for your recent bike trip to the grocery store you prevented 2 lbs of CO2 from enetering the atmosphere. However if you earn negative 2 points that means you did emit 2 lbs of CO2 into the atmosphere.

Please note that this is not intented to be your actaul carbon footprint. On our game you can offset your driving emmsions by biking an equal amount thus setting your points to 0. In the real world your tail pipe emissions enter the atmosphere every time you turn your car on, and riding you bike does not take it back.
